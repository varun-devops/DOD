Tips for Best Results
Sit quietly for 2 minutes before any vitals test to ensure accurate readings.
Remove tight sleeves from your upper arm before the BP cuff test.
Do not eat or drink 30 minutes before the blood sugar (glucometer) test.
Keep your hands relaxed and steady during ECG electrode attachment.
Use the earphones provided in the kiosk for teleconsultation audio clarity.
Always log out after your session — your health data is private and encrypted.

Step-by-Step Guides

How to Use the DOD Kiosk

Simple instructions for every key feature — anyone can use it in minutes

01

How to do ECG

  1. Select ECG Test on the home screen
  2. Attach the ECG electrode pads as shown on-screen diagram
  3. Sit still and breathe normally for 30 seconds
  4. View your 12-lead ECG result instantly on screen
  5. Download or print your ECG report
Watch Video
02

How to do BP Monitoring

  1. Select Blood Pressure from the main menu
  2. Slide your arm into the BP cuff on the kiosk
  3. Sit upright, feet flat, and relax for a moment
  4. Press Start — cuff inflates automatically
  5. Systolic, diastolic and heart rate shown on screen
Watch Video
03

How to use Digital Stethoscope

  1. Select Stethoscope from the device menu
  2. Pick up the digital stethoscope from the dock
  3. Place the chest piece on your chest as directed
  4. Stay still while heart & lung sounds are recorded
  5. Doctor hears the sounds live during teleconsult
Watch Video
04

How to Download Your Prescription

  1. Complete your consultation with the doctor
  2. Go to My Reports on the home screen
  3. Select the consultation date to view your prescription
  4. Tap Download PDF or Print
  5. Scan the QR code to get it on your phone instantly
Watch Video
05

How to Book Consultation with Doctors

  1. Tap Book Appointment on the main menu
  2. Select speciality and preferred doctor
  3. Choose available date and time slot
  4. Confirm with your mobile number (OTP verification)
  5. Receive confirmation and reminder on your phone
